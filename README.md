## About This Portfolio

Welcome to my AI in Healthcare portfolio. This repository showcases my coursework, written assignments, and academic projects completed in AI in Healthcare at Houston City College. The purpose of this portfolio is to document my learning journey, demonstrate my growth throughout the course, and present my work in a professional and organized format.

This course explores how artificial intelligence can be applied in healthcare to improve patient care, diagnostics, decision support, data analysis, and health system innovation. Through this portfolio, I reflect on key concepts, practical applications, ethical considerations, and future opportunities for AI in medicine.

## About Me

My name is Hisham Macaraya, and I am deeply interested in artificial intelligence, healthcare innovation, and the practical use of technology to solve real-world problems. I created this portfolio to highlight the work I completed during this course and to show how my understanding of AI in healthcare developed across multiple topics.

## Course Overview

AI in Healthcare is a course designed to connect AI concepts with real healthcare challenges. Throughout the semester, I explored foundational and advanced topics including:

- AI, machine learning, and deep learning in healthcare
- Medical terminology for AI applications
- Healthcare datasets and clinical data interpretation
- Clinical decision support systems
- AI diagnostic tools
- Risk modeling and prognosis
- Population health analytics
- Natural language processing in healthcare
- Reinforcement learning in clinical settings
- Human-centered AI design
- Conversational agents
- Federated learning
- Ethics and cybersecurity
- The future of AI in healthcare
